Assessment Criteria

Failure to provide code, documentation or demo will result in a mark of 0%. You will be penalised an absolute value of 10% for every day that your assignment is late. The assignment must be demo-ed in the labs.

Note: You may use provided IDWD_Assignment_Document_Template.doc
